The blueprint for establishing and scaling a Data & Analytics Center of Excellence within
a large enterprise — built from direct experience leading the practice at The Home Depot.

**COE pillars:**
- Federated team model balancing central standards with domain autonomy
- Standardized intake, prioritization, and delivery frameworks
- Reusable asset library and knowledge management system
- Talent development and upskilling programs
- Governance and data product ownership model
