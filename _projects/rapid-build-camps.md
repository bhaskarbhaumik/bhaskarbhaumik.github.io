Rapid Build Camps are time-boxed, high-intensity innovation sprints designed to take enterprise AI
ideas from concept to working MVP in 4–8 weeks.

**What makes them different:**
- Pre-built accelerator library cuts discovery and scaffolding time in half
- Structured playbooks for repeatable delivery across industries
- Embedded responsible AI and governance checkpoints
- Direct client stakeholder engagement throughout the sprint
